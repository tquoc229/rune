# Project Strategy: HR Trends 2026 & Agentic AI

## Objective
Establish thought leadership in the HR space for 2026 by emphasizing the shift from basic AI adoption to Agentic AI and Systems Thinking. Challenge the status quo of HR professionals merely learning to use AI tools, pushing them to become architects of self-governing AI systems.

## Target Audience
- HR Directors, CHROs, and BOM (Board of Management) members.
- Forward-thinking HR professionals looking beyond ChatGPT and prompt engineering.

## KPI / Success Metrics
- High engagement rate (comments/shares) from C-level and Directors on social platforms (Facebook/LinkedIn).
- Positioning "Thanh Nguyen" as the leading analytical voice in HR tech.

## Core Message (The Hook)
Stop training employees how to use AI tools. Start redesigning your organization around autonomous AI Agents. The future HR leader is a behavioral system architect, not an IT trainer.

## Guardrails
- MUST strictly adhere to the `thanh-nguyen` persona.
- MUST use a dispassionate, analytical tone (The Stoic style).
- MUST NOT use generic AI buzzwords (e.g., "kỷ nguyên AI", "chìa khóa vàng", "giải pháp tối ưu").
- MUST enforce "Hard Truth Imperative" - deliver reality checks without sugarcoating.
